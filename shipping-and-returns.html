How to Order

Browse Our Listings: Explore our website to discover a variety of products available for purchase. We now offer both in-stock items and special order products sourced from trusted Japanese marketplaces and retail stores.


Place an Order: If an item is labeled "In Stock," it is available for immediate shipping. Products marked "Special Order" will be purchased after you complete payment. If you’re using our proxy-buying service, we will source the item specifically for you. Please read more about how our proxy-buying service works.


Deposit & Confirmation: For special order and proxy-buying items, we require a deposit to proceed with your request. This allows us to secure the item on your behalf. If the item is no longer available, we will notify you and issue a refund.


We Source & Purchase: Our team will locate the product in Japan from online marketplaces like Mercari, Yahoo Auctions, or retail stores. Once secured, we inspect the item and prepare it for shipment.


Shipping & Delivery: For in-stock items, we ship immediately using reliable international carriers. Special order and proxy-buying items will be shipped as soon as we receive them. Tracking details will be provided so you can monitor your package’s journey from Japan to your doorstep.
