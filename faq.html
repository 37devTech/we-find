3. Can I cancel or change my order after purchasing?

Orders cannot be canceled or modified once they have been shipped. Please double-check your order details before completing your purchase.

4. What happens if my item arrives damaged?

We thoroughly inspect all items before shipping to ensure they are in the best possible condition. If your item is damaged during shipping, you must file a claim directly with the delivery service.

6. How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on the destination and shipping method. Customers will receive a tracking number to monitor their shipment.

7. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.

8. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal and Stripe for secure transactions.

9. Are there any additional fees for proxy-buying?

Yes, a service fee may apply depending on the item and sourcing process. Full details will be provided at the time of purchase.
